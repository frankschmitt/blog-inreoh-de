--- layout: post title: Replacing Ctrl+M with newline date: '2013-05-06T08:42:00.002-07:00' author: Frank Schmitt tags: modified_time: '2013-05-06T08:44:29.572-07:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-6373343020354235841.post-2634478455941740612 blogger_orig_url: http://inreoh.blogspot.com/2013/05/replacing-ctrlm-with-newline.html --- Differences in line endings (CR vs CR+LF vs ...) are a constant PITA. Here's one quick hack I used to fix some Haskell source code copied from StackOverflow and pasted via pbpaste:

cat udp_syslog_server.hs | tr "[^M]" "[\n]"
