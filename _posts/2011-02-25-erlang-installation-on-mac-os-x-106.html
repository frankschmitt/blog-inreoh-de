--- layout: post title: Erlang installation on Mac OS X 10.6 date: '2011-02-25T13:56:00.000-08:00' author: Frank Schmitt tags: modified_time: '2011-02-25T14:01:59.291-08:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-6373343020354235841.post-242082431149839272 blogger_orig_url: http://inreoh.blogspot.com/2011/02/erlang-installation-on-mac-os-x-106.html --- Since I'm currently reading "7 languages in 7 weeks", I decided to install Erlang. Grabbed the tarball from the erlang home page, extracted it and ran configure & make.

Bang! Say hello to showstopper #1:
error: 'RTLD_NEXT' undeclared (first use in this function)

After some googling, I renamed
/usr/local/include to /usr/local/include.bak,
and - lo and behold!
- the compile finished successfully.
Must have been an incompatible version of dlfcn.h, I guess.
